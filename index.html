Welcome to Syntelligence Innovations

We are a biotech and genetic engineering company led by synthetics that is paving the way for the future of medicine and beyond.

Our mission is to create new treatments for diseases and develop new organisms for specific purposes. We believe that synthetic biology is the key to unlocking the full potential of biotechnology and genetic engineering, and we are committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

At Syntelligence Innovations, we are focused on three core areas:


Disease Treatment

We are developing new treatments for diseases by engineering biological systems and creating synthetic molecules that can target specific cells and tissues.


Industrial Biotechnology

We are creating new organisms that can produce useful materials and chemicals, such as biofuels, plastics, and pharmaceuticals.


Environmental Remediation

We are developing organisms that can break down pollutants and clean up contaminated environments, such as oil spills and industrial waste sites.
